T1 Supernatural Powers v1.1

GLOBAL RULE
- Every power is explicitly categorized as one of:
  - Passive
  - Active
- Passive powers are always in effect and do not require activation.
- Active powers require activation.
- For every Active power level, the following fields are mandatory:
  - Action
  - Mana Cost
  - Range
- In addition, every Active power level MUST explicitly specify exactly one of:
  - Duration
  - Concentration
  - Instant
- Passive power levels MUST explicitly state:
  - Duration: Passive
- No power effect may be left temporally undefined.
